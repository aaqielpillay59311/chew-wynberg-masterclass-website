Why Chew

More than a morning coffee.

We built Chew to be the warmest room on Church Street — the kind of place where the barista knows your order and the croissants are still warm.

Coffee done properly

Espresso, flat whites, red cappuccinos and rooibos lattes — pulled with care, never rushed. Your daily ritual, the way it should taste.

Baked this morning

Buttery croissants, fresh scones, sandwiches and hearty breakfasts. If it left our kitchen, it left it warm.

Women-owned & homely

A local, women-owned café with a “design a sandwich for the whole party” kind of hospitality. You’re not a table number here.

The Chew feeling

Warm walls, good light, better company.

Cocoa-toned walls, hanging plants and a marble counter you’ll want to linger at. Come for the flat white, stay for the corner that feels like your living room.
